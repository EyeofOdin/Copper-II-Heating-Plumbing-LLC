About Copper II Heating & Plumbing

Established in 2006, Copper II Heating & Plumbing LLC has been providing quality heating, cooling, and plumbing services to the Long Island City community and beyond. With our team of skilled technicians, we deliver reliable solutions to both residential and commercial clients.

We take pride in our commitment to excellence, fair pricing, and customer satisfaction. Our team is licensed, insured, and continuously trained in the latest industry technologies and best practices.
